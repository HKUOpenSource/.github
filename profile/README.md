Welcome to **STAR Open Source Lab@HKU**, led by **Prof. Yuanwei Liu** in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Hong Kong.

We build and share research code, simulation tools, datasets, and reproducible examples for the next generation of wireless systems. Our work sits at the intersection of **6G communications**, **foundation AI models**, **networked intelligence**, and **advanced antenna technologies**.

## What You Will Find Here

- Open-source research platforms for 6G and AI-native communications.
- Research code released with papers, tutorials, and technical reports.
- Reproducible simulation frameworks for wireless communication and sensing.
- Educational material for students and researchers entering 6G and AI-native communications.

## Collaboration

We welcome collaboration with researchers, students, industry partners, and open-source contributors interested in future wireless systems.

Good contributions include:

- Reproducing and validating published results.
- Reporting issues with clear environment details and minimal examples.
- Improving documentation, examples, and tutorials.
- Extending baselines with well-tested implementations.
- Proposing new research directions connected to 6G, AI, sensing, and communications.
